The Big Day

We are booked in to the Caernarfon registry office at 11am and can't wait to see everyone there.
The ceremony will last around 30 minutes and we have a photographer that will take some photos during the ceremony.

After the ceremony, we will get a few photos with our friends and family, both at the registry office and at Caernarfon Castle.
As I'm sure you will agree, Wales has some of the most beautiful landscapes and it would be a shame to not take advantage of this.

As a result, this is where we will leave you to go off and do your own thing and grab some food for a few hours.
We will head off into the mountains with our photographer and have various photos planned (you are welcome to join us on this excursion).
Once we have our photography trip planned, we will update this page so you can decide if you would like to come and find us.

Once we finish with our photographer, we will then make our way to "insert name of pub here" for a lovely meal and one or two bevvies.

As a thank you for coming all this way to witness our special day, we will pick up the food bill.
Unfortunately, as we are on a budget, we just ask for you to get your own drinks.
