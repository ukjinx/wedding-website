The Big Day

We are booked in to the Caernarfon registry office at 11am and can't wait to see everyone there.
The ceremony will last around 30 minutes and we have a photographer that will take some photos during the ceremony.

After the ceremony, we will get a few photos with our friends and family, both at the registry office and at Caernarfon Castle.
As I'm sure you will agree, Wales has some of the most beautiful landscapes and it would be a shame to not take advantage of this.

As a result, this is where we will leave you to go off and do your own thing and grab some food for a few hours.
We will head off into the mountains with our photographer and have various photos planned (you are welcome to join us on this excursion).
Once we have our photography trip planned, we will update this page so you can decide if you would like to come and find us.

Once we finish with our photographer, we will then make our way to "insert name of pub here" for a lovely meal and one or two bevvies.

As a thank you for coming all this way to witness our special day, we will pick up the food bill.
Unfortunately, as we are on a budget, we just ask for you to get your own drinks.

Where to stay?

We are planning on heading to Snowdonia the weekend before the wedding (approximately 10th September).


As we will be in the area for an extended amount of time, we will be planning on booking a cottage at Red Dragon Holidays.
We would love for everyone to stay at Red Dragon with us and, if we book at the same time then we can get a 10% group booking discount.
The benefit of this is that we can carry on celebrating through the night and week with cheap food and drink from Tesco.
If you are interested in this, please check their website at the available cottages/Pods and let us know ASAP as spaces are filling up quickly. Myself and Donna will be booking the Sygun cottage.

The website is: https://www.reddragonholidays.co.uk/

We appreciate that self contained cottages may not be everyones cup of tea so there is no pressure if you don't wish to hire a cottage.
If you would rather look for a B&B, you can find plenty of accomodation in the local village of Beddgelert, which is near Red Dragon Holidays.
Alternatively, you can also find accomodation in Llanberis, Porthmadog or Caernarfon.
We are unable to provide recommendations at these places as we generally stay at Red Dragon.
